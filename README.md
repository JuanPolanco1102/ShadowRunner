üéÆ About the Game
The Story
You are a warrior trapped within the depths of an ancestral fortress. To escape, you must navigate through crumbling dungeons, ruined gardens, and perilous towers. Along the way, you must collect energy fragments from ancient artifacts to unlock the path forward while surviving deadly traps and patrolling enemies.

Gameplay Loop
Objective: Reach the exit door of each level.

Challenge: Collect energy fragments and maintain your 3 lives.

Progression: Traverse through checkpoints and unlock abilities like the Double Jump.

‚ú® Key Features
Classic 2D Physics: Responsive side-scrolling movement with smooth acceleration.

Warrior Skills: Master the single and double jump to reach difficult platforms.

Dynamic Hazards: Dodge static traps (spikes, saws) and outmaneuver patrolling enemies.

Item Collection: Gather Energy Fragments for score and Hearts for extra lives.

Retro Aesthetic: Pixel art visuals combined with atmospheric sound design.

Progression System: Checkpoints save your progress within levels; UI tracks time and collection stats.
